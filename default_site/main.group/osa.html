Online Safety Association

Environmental Health & Safety Training for Higher Education

The Online Safety Association is a professional cooperative dedicated to advancing campus safety program administration through technology.

The OSA develops quality online safety training with an emphasis on research and laboratory needs.

100% of OSA participant dues are dedicated to the creation of exceptional training for the OSA member community.

Participation is offered exclusively to colleges, universities, and higher education systems across the country.
