While organizations and working environments are unique, the need for safety training isn’t.

Training Alignment

Identifying the right mix of courses to bring your workforce into compliance is simple. We use a combination of Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) & North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code matching.

Delivery & Access

Log in & start training—it’s that easy. Courses are delivered by Vivid’s Safety Training System (LMS), cloud software you don’t have to install. Courses are 24-7 accessible for any employee at any location across your organization.

Training Quality

All courses perform to Subject Content Object Reference Model (SCORM) & Aviation Industry Computer-Based Training Committee (AICC) standards, and are available in those formats for organizations with an existing learning management system.
