Online Safety Training for a Safer, Smarter Workforce.

 

Vivid Learning Systems provides OSHA safety training online and offers a wide range of online courses for companies looking to keep workers safe.

Vivid’s online safety courses offer the content and value of classroom training programs needed to keep workers and employees safe, all at the same time saving on travel time and cost.

We know that effective training delivers information in unique ways, creating lasting memorability to reach workers and influence behaviors.

In addition to OSHA courses, Vivid is a provider of the only MSHA approved course collection, the only electrical worker training program online, Department of Transportation (DOT) and much more.

Take a look below or demo our courses to see how Vivid’s online safety training can help you train a safer workforce.
