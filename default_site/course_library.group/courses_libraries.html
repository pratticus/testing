Online Safety Training for a Safer, Smarter Workforce.

Vivid Learning Systems is a provider of online safety training.

With 20-minute courses covering OSHA’s General Industry training requirements, along with online training for Manufacturing, Construction, Electrical Workers (1910.269), Mine Safety & Health Administration (MSHA), and Department of Transportation (DOT) safety standards, you can cover your entire workforce.

Designed to maximize the training experience through interactivity, Vivid’s online safety courses promote engagement that creates lasting memorability, influencing safe working behaviors.

Beyond compliance courses, Vivid offers hundreds of diverse training options designed to reinforce safety concepts and reach each employee level of participation with an ‘all-of-the-above’ training strategy. Optional content includes printable supplemental materials like safety checklists, short safety tip videos for ‘toolbox’ style training activity, and new 5-Minute Courses for refresher training on annual safety requirements. All resources may be assigned through Vivid’s Safety Training System (LMS).

Vivid online safety courses work on mobile devices, and are compatible with most Learning Management Systems (LMS), supporting both Aviation Industry Computer-Based Training Committee (AICC) & Sharable Content Object Reference Model (SCORM) delivery.
